### Key concepts

The AI Pocket Book serves as a concise reference guide covering essential AI concepts, techniques, and applications in an accessible format for quick consultation. The book provides high-level overviews of machine learning types including supervised, unsupervised, and reinforcement learning, plus modern deep learning approaches with transformers and foundation models. Core concepts include understanding when to apply different AI techniques, recognizing data requirements and quality considerations, and navigating the AI landscape from classical algorithms through modern neural architectures with practical guidance on selecting appropriate approaches for specific problems.

### Who should read it and why

This book targets busy professionals, managers, and developers needing quick AI reference material without extensive technical depth, suitable for those making technology decisions, communicating with technical teams, or needing refreshers on AI concepts. The compact format appeals to readers wanting foundational knowledge without commitment to lengthy textbooks, business leaders evaluating AI opportunities, and technical professionals from other domains needing AI literacy. The material assumes general technical familiarity but explains concepts accessibly for readers across experience levels.

### Practical applications

The book provides quick reference for AI terminology, algorithm selection criteria, and implementation considerations across common use cases. Readers gain practical frameworks for evaluating AI project feasibility, understanding tradeoffs between different approaches, and asking informed questions of technical teams. The material covers recognizing appropriate applications for AI, identifying data requirements, understanding model limitations, and navigating vendor solutions versus custom development decisions with guidance applicable across industries and problem domains.
